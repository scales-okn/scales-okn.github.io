# How to Use This Site

Welcome to the SCALES OKN documentation site! This is a compendium of various resources that we think might be valuable to our users, collaborators, and the general public. In the Documentation section, you can access traditional documentation-style outlines of our code, including our PACER scraper and parser. In the Guides section, you'll find more casual material that we've written on the ins and outs of our software tools and research work. These guides are an effort to share important insights that don't quite fit into the low-level technical focus of the documentation pages, but that we believe will enrich your experience with the SCALES-OKN platform.

As we expand our research work and make more of our results available to the public, we'll be updating this site with additional guides and links, so make sure to check back regularly to see what we're up to. And if you have any specific questions or requests, feel free to reach out directly; we're a small, responsive team that's always looking for new collaborations and partnerships, so we'd love to hear from you! You can find our contact information here.

Note that this site does not host the SCALES codebase. To view and download our code, you can visit our Github repositories, which are listed here.
